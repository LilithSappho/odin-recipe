Recipe to make Porotta Beef

Porotta and beef is the main food in Kerala. Keralites even view this as pride

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Porotta
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Chilli powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Salt
  • Coconut oil

Preparation

  1. Wash beef pieces well
  2. Cook with salt
  3. Head coconut oil
  4. Add tomato
  5. Add Chilli Powder, Garam masala
  6. Mix well
  7. Serve the prepared beef with Porotta
